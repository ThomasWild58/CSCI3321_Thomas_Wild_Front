The Rules of the game are:

1. Decide who is going to be X and who is going to be O.

2. Take turns marking in the grid your corresponding letter either "X or O"

3. First to get 3 of their letters in a row horizontally, diagonally, or veritically wins the game.

4. If all squares are full before there is a win the game will be declared a draw.

5. To begin the game press the start button and to replay the game after winning or if there is a draw.
